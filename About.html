About

Bean and Brew are a small local chain of cafes based around Leeds. Despite being known as a cafe, our services also provide baking lessons, as well as takeaways and restaurant-like dishes. Being one of the first in the UK to use all organic milk and fair-trade cofee, each drink we serve is made exactly to your style.
